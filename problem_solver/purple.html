Problem Solvers is a private peer group focused on empowering high-output achievers to accelerate their professional growth

At Problem Solvers, we pair cohorts of 10 -12 individuals with high-profile leaders and experts with experience in focused areas relevant to the cohort. Peer groups meet regularly and are facilitated by PurpleCrest’s Management Consultants to create deep connections and hold each other accountable for continued growth.

What do you get as a Problem Solver?

Membership dues at INR 1999 per month, are paid annually and provide members with access to all sponsored events, forums, cohort discussions, and the PurpleCrest Community Directory

  • Opportunities to connect and contact peers outside of your cohort or industry.
  • Access to the Problem Solvers database
  • Access to your vetted, industry-specific cohort of peers
  • Facilitated monthly meetings with your peer group
  • Access to our private Problem Solvers forum
  • Private Q&A sessions and discussions with business leaders
  • Exclusive event invites (Toronto, Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad)
  • Ask questions, discuss, and get help when you need it
